About Pranexus

Pranexus Automations Pvt. Ltd. is a technology-driven company specializing in Industrial Automation and Weighing Systems. We deliver end-to-end automation solutions that include hardware, software, and integration for manufacturing, logistics, and warehousing industries.

Our mission is to empower businesses by optimizing operational efficiency, reducing errors, and enabling smarter decision-making through innovative automation technologies. We combine advanced machinery, intelligent software, and IoT-enabled systems to provide reliable, scalable, and future-ready solutions.

With years of experience and a team of dedicated engineers, we have successfully implemented projects across diverse sectors, including automotive, pharmaceuticals, FMCG, logistics, and e-commerce. Our solutions are designed to be flexible, modular, and customized to meet the unique requirements of each client.

At Pranexus, we value innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction. Our goal is to transform industrial operations by integrating cutting-edge technologies such as AI, machine learning, and smart sensors, ensuring our clients stay ahead in a competitive market.
